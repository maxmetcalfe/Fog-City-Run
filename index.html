Fog City Run

A weekly running race in San Francisco, CA


  When: The race is every Wednesday @ 6:30pm. Rain or shine. Best to arrive at 6:15pm.

  Where: We leave from The Blue Light - 1979 Union St, San Francisco, CA 94123.

  How do I register? Registration is upon arrival. $5.00 cash. We usually don't have change.

  What is the course? A hilly 5km(ish) route through the Marina neighborhood and the Presidio (Map).

  How many people come? 5-20. You can view the race results here and on Strava here.

  A locked closet is available for any extra belongings.
